# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard** built using Power BI.
The dashboard helps businesses analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer segments, and regional insights to support **data-driven decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* To analyze retail sales data and extract meaningful business insights
* To visualize key metrics such as revenue, profit, and orders
* To help stakeholders identify trends and optimize business strategies

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* **Power BI** (Dashboard & Visualization)
* **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* **DAX** (Calculated Measures)
* **Excel/CSV Dataset**

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 KPI Metrics

* Total Sales: 88.37K
* Total Profit: 15.44K
* Total Orders: 1130

### 🔹 Sales Analysis

* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category & Sub-Category
* Monthly Sales Trend

### 🔹 Profit Analysis

* Monthly Profit Trend
* Profit Distribution by State (Map Visualization)

### 🔹 Customer & Business Insights

* Sales by Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
* Sales by Payment Mode
* Sales by Ship Mode

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* The **West region** contributes the highest share of sales
* **Technology category** generates high revenue
* Sales show a peak during **October–December (seasonal trend)**
* Majority of customers belong to the **Consumer segment**
